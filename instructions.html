Instructions Assignment

This project provides step-by-step instructions for creating a Google Ads account from scratch. The goal is to make the setup process easy and safe for beginners who have never used digital advertising before.

Assignment Overview

In this ENC 3241 assignment, I focused on clarity, organization, and accessibility. The instructions walk users through each step, from visiting the Google Ads website to skipping the automatic campaign creation flow, so they do not accidentally spend money before they are ready. The document uses headings, numbered steps, and brief explanations to support a wide range of readers.
